'Viva La Us Are Negative' Trials details

what takes place

This event will have three trails, each having a different style.

Mods will judge you on how you did and give you 1-5 points. who ever has the most amount of points win.


First Place Prizes: First Place Prizes: Wall Of Fame, Wall of Fame role, Decides Next Event Theme, Custom Role, Judge in Next Event, Shoutout in Announcement

Second Place Prizes: Custom Role, Shoutout in Announcement, Wall Of Fame, Custom Emoji

Third Place Prizes: Custom Role, Shoutout in Announcement, Access to test channel

Trial One:

Click to view Trial One instructions

The Tribunal has declared: You are not who you seem.
All participants must craft their mask. Their false identity. Their inner Negative.

Instructions for Participants:

Welcome to Trial One.
To survive, you must forge your “Negative Identity.”
Create an alter ego that embodies negativity, chaos, absurdity, or pure cursedness.

You may express this identity in any format you choose:

  • A drawing (even stick figures and MS Paint are allowed)
  • A short description or backstory
  • Etc. — let your cursed creativity shine

Then, post your entry in the form.

Deadline: July 1st, 2025
