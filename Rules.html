Server Rules

  1. No War Crimes
    Don’t do seriously messed up stuff (child porn, threats, assault, harassment). Report bad stuff in #❗reports. Mods will act.
    But when it’s clearly joking and no one takes offense to it, then it should be fine—unless it’s REALLY bad.

  2. Lounge vs Venting
    • #🪸lounge: Chill and chat. No targeting culture, religion, or groups.
    • #🤬venting-and-oversharing: Say whatever you need but no harassment or fights.

  3. Mod DMs
    Mods/admins may DM you about server stuff. Junior mods can’t DM first. Weird messages? Tell another mod.

  4. Suggestions
    Got ideas? Drop them in #🗣suggestions. We might actually listen.

  5. Announcements
    Important updates go in #🎉announcements and #⬆server-updates. Check them or miss out.

  6. Horny Jail
    Post horny stuff or spicy links? You get the Horny Role and go to #🥵horny-jail. Keep it mostly clean.

  7. Channels for Fun
    #😼art-uwu, #📦memes, #😞tumblr, and #🪱self-promo (30-min cooldown) for your creative stuff.

  8. Voice Channels
    Two open voice channels. Act decent or get muted. Mods have their own channel (no invites).

  9. Reaction Roles
    Grab roles in #🤪reaction-roles to customize your server experience.

Play nice, don’t be a jerk, and enjoy the chaos. Break rules, get kicked. Simple.
